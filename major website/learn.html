Learn About Grocery Items

Rice

Rice is a staple food in many countries. It is rich in carbohydrates and given energy.

Wheat Flour

Wheat flour is used to make chapatis and other baked goods. It is a source of fiber.

Lentils (Dal)

Lentils are a great source of protein for vegetables. They come in many varieties.

vegetables

Fresh vegetables are essential for vitamins and minerals. Eat a variety every day.
